SHORT REPLIES


    Used to:

  • show that you are listening

  • keep conversation going

1. Mmhmm

It’s been raining so much lately.
Mmhm
I hope the sun comes out soon.

2. Aww


I lost my cell phone.
Aww...
I hope I find it soon.

3. Cool


We went to the movies this weekend.
Oh, cool!
We saw the new Star Wars film.

4. Oh, interesting!


I’m studying to be a nurse.
Oh, interesting!
I plan to graduate in June.

5. I'm sorry to hear that


I’m not feeling very well.
I'm sorry to hear that!
I think I have a cold.

6. That's awesome


I got a new job!
That’s awesome!
Yeah, I’m really excited.
